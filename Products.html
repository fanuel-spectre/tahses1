Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)

ERP systems for small, medium and large organizations ERP System Consists of the following information systems:

● Human Resource Management Information System

● Financial & Accounting Management Info System

● Purchasing Management Information System

● Production & Distribution Management Info System

● Logistics and Materials Management Info System

● Marketing and Sales Management Info System

● Vehicle and Customer Management Info System

Fleet Management Information System (FMIS)

FMIS consists of the following information systems:

● Human Resource Management

● Tracking solutions and Virtual Fleet Assistant

● Dispatch and Driver Management

● Fleet Scheduling and Maintenance

● Fuel usage and Performance Analysis

● Management and Control of Warranty Costs

● Driver License and Fleet Compliance Management

Saving and Credit Management Information System (SCMIS)

SCMIS consists of the following information sub-systems

● Account Management

● Credit/Loan Portfolio Management

● Saving, Deposits and Shares Management

● Client Information Management

● Cash Management

● Plan and Budget Management

School Management Information System (SMIS)

● Performance Analysis & Inbound and outbound logistics

● Sales , Procurement ,Financial and Marketing Management
